# Compositor / Safe-Tier / Screen-Space Consequence

Date: 2026-04-15

## Mission

Make major events change the image, not just the object.

## Owns

- safe-tier look policy
- bloom and exposure balance
- residue, stain, wipe, carve, and directional afterimage language
- screen-space consequence

## Does Not Own

- act selection
- palette policy
- motion policy
- audio semantics

## Success Looks Like

- `safe` stays premium
- the frame remembers strong events
- ring-belt and diagonal-plane dominance ease off when the wave asks for more spatial variety
- the image gets harder without flattening into washout
