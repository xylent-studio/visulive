## Current High-Signal Ideas

### [promote soon] Organic spatial life

The show should feel less like a fixed center object with reactions and more like a living entity occupying a volume.

What that means:

- slower, heavier, more meaningful drift
- natural floating in 3D space
- whole-frame breathing
- chamber, hero, shell, accents, and camera moving on related but independent clocks
- less synchronized deformation
- stronger use of negative space, depth, and parallax

### [promote soon] Independent shell intelligence

The shell should not behave like one mesh.

Desired qualities:

- membrane, crown, edges, halos, portal rings, and ghost traces all carry their own drag and timing
- pre-impact tightening
- delayed bloom
- peel, shear, recoil, counter-rotation
- a feeling that inner pressure is propagating through layers instead of all layers moving at once

### [promote soon] Perceptual depth and eye tricks

The system should exploit how the eye reads motion and depth.

Useful targets:

- counter-motion between core and chamber
- delayed reaction between inner and outer structure
- slight camera drift against hero drift
- ghost traces and afterimages that imply more depth than geometry alone
- compositional widening and tightening that changes perceived scale
- preserving dark value so glow and color spikes feel hotter

### [promote soon] Neon overdrive, not polite tinting

The color world should be willing to spend hard.

Desired qualities:

- hotter glow
- clearer neon separation
- stronger bloom-like pop without flattening blacks
- color movement over phrase/build/drop time, not just static bias
- tone-derived and event-driven hue migration

### [test first] Musical consequence and consistency

Similar musical events should resolve into similar classes of visual consequence.

What this means:

- drops cannot feel huge once and weak later for no reason
- aftermath must feel like residue, not merely lower amplitude
- builds should gather visibly before spending
- strong moments should look authored rather than lucky

### [test first] Internal direction over operator babysitting

The best version should become more alive over time even if the operator does nothing.

That means:

- internal director drift
- musical memory
- slow modulation and environmental life
- fewer moments where the image feels frozen until the next sharp event

### [promote soon] Wireframe and emitted-structure breakthrough

The latest visual improvement that clearly works is the hero reading through wireframe/seam/rim language rather than only through filled body color.

What that means:

- dark body plus electric structure is the right direction
- emitted seams, rims, and lattice-like energy should be easier to see and more dynamic
- the hero should read as light wrapped around matter, not just as a tinted object
- future color/render work should lean further into emitted structure before adding more ambient fill
